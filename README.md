# 🎯 Placement Preparation Hub

My campus placement preparation workspace — interactive practice tools I built, company-wise preparation plans, and structured DSA practice for the 2026–27 placement season.

## 🛠️ Interactive Tools (built from scratch)

Self-contained HTML apps — open any of them directly in a browser, no setup needed.

| Tool | What it does |
|------|--------------|
| [`mental_math_trainer.html`](Placement%20Training/mental_math_trainer.html) | Timed mental-math trainer for aptitude rounds — speed arithmetic under pressure |
| [`dsa_a2z_drill.html`](Placement%20Training/dsa_a2z_drill.html) | DSA drill tracker following an A2Z problem protocol |
| [`dsa_drill_expanded.html`](Placement%20Training/dsa_drill_expanded.html) | Expanded edition — 144-problem structured drill with progress tracking |

## 🏢 Company-Wise Preparation

For each company I interviewed with, I built a targeted prep plan: predicted online-assessment mock papers, round-by-round strategy, and research notes gathered from seniors and past candidates.

| Company | Materials |
|---------|-----------|
| Morgan Stanley | 1-day OA prep plan + mock paper, interview research notes |
| Presidio | Prep HQ dashboard, 60-min aptitude sprint, predicted OA mock |
| Tredence | Prep HQ with OA countdown plan |
| Swym | 1-night OA prep plan + mock paper |

## 📚 DSA Practice

`Placement Training/DSA/` — topic-wise solutions written while drilling, organized by pattern.

## 💡 Approach

Rather than passively reading prep material, I turned my preparation into a system: every company gets researched, every weak area gets a purpose-built drill tool, and everything is tracked. This repo is the result.
