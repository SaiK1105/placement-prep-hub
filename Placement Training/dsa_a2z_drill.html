Roadmap

The first ~60% of this order covers ~90% of campus OA questions. Click a topic to make it current; click again to see its problems. Trees is pre-selected — Tredence's OA on Monday includes tree questions.

Re-solve queue

Problems you needed help with, back for a from-scratch attempt. Clearing this queue daily is where retrieval speed is actually built.

Pattern book

One line per solved problem — this list is what you skim before any OA. Aim to collect ~30 patterns, not 450 solutions.

The protocol

  • 25 minutes, timer on, always. The timer is the training — unlimited-time solving is a different (easier) sport than the one exams play.
  • Stuck at ~10 min? Hit "show approach", read it, close your eyes, implement from memory. Never copy code.
  • Be honest with the three buttons. "Solved myself" only counts if you wrote it without the approach. Assisted and failed problems return in 2–3 days — that's a feature, not a punishment.
  • One pattern line per solve. Read it back weekly. You're building a mental index, not a completed checklist.
  • 3 problems a day. One easy warm-up, two mediums. Sustainable beats heroic — 6-7 weeks covers everything campus placements ask.
  • Bring beaten problems to Claude. Paste your failed attempt in chat for an interviewer-style review.
