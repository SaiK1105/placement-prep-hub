The protocol

  • 25 minutes, timer on. Stuck at ~10? Read the approach, close it, implement from memory.
  • Honest buttons. "Solved myself" means zero peeks. Assisted → back in 2 days; failed → back tomorrow.
  • 3 problems a day — one easy, two medium. ~7 weeks covers all 144.
  • Pattern book weekly review. The index in your head is the product; problems are just the reps.
  • Failed twice? Bring your attempt to Claude for an interviewer-style teardown.
